    Singular value decomposition

    Mar 04, 20254 min read

    A=UΣVT where

    • A is m×n
    • U: An m×m Orthogonal matrix matrix whose columns are the left singular vectors of A.
    • Σ: A diagonal m×n matrix containing the singular values of A in descending order.
    • VT: The transpose of an n×n orthogonal matrix, where the columns are the right singular vectors of A.

    Steps

    1. calculate AAT
    2. calculate Eigenvalues of AAT by solving det(AAT−λI)=0, where I is identify matrix
    3. then Σ is δ from those λ
    4. calculate V right singular vectors(should be unit vectors), for each λ, (ATA−λI)v=0
    5. calculate U left singular vectors(should be unit vectors), for each λ, (AAT−λI)u=0
